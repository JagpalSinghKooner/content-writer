### PAA Questions (7)

1. **Q:** Do ADHD apps actually work?
   **Answer Approach:** Honest: limited research, but some show promise. Position as supplement to support, not replacement. Cite EndeavorRx FDA approval and research gaps.

2. **Q:** What is the best app for a child with ADHD?
   **Answer Approach:** "It depends on what your child needs." Break down by need: focus, emotional regulation, task management, sleep, sensory support.

3. **Q:** Is there a free app for ADHD?
   **Answer Approach:** Yes, list free options. But also explain why some paid apps may be worth it. Introduce HushAway® as an option for sound support.

4. **Q:** What is the FDA-approved ADHD game?
   **Answer Approach:** EndeavorRx. Explain what it is, age range, evidence, and that it's a supplement not replacement for other support.

5. **Q:** How can I help my ADHD child focus without medication?
   **Answer Approach:** Apps are one tool among many. Mention structure, sensory support, emotional regulation. Position sound therapy as complementary support.

6. **Q:** Are there apps for ADHD emotional regulation?
   **Answer Approach:** Yes. List options: Zones of Regulation, Choiceworks, Goally Mood Tuner, mindfulness apps. Position HushAway® as passive emotional regulation support through sound.

7. **Q:** What sounds help ADHD children focus?
   **Answer Approach:** Brown noise, white noise, binaural beats. Cite research. This is HushAway®'s core expertise.
