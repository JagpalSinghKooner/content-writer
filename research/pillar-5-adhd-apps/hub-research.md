## Section 5: People Also Ask Questions

**Status:** [x] Complete

**Source:** Google SERP for "ADHD apps"
**Date Captured:** 2026-01-28

### PAA Questions (7)

1. **Q:** What is the best app for a child with ADHD?
   **Answer Approach:** There is no single best app. Match the app to the child's specific needs. Start with regulation before focus.

2. **Q:** What is the FDA approved ADHD app?
   **Answer Approach:** EndeavorRx is the only FDA-approved app. Explain what it does and its limitations.

3. **Q:** Are there free ADHD apps that actually work?
   **Answer Approach:** Yes, several free options exist. Be honest about limitations of free vs paid.

4. **Q:** What sounds help ADHD children focus?
   **Answer Approach:** White noise, brown noise, binaural beats. This is HushAway's core positioning.

5. **Q:** Do ADHD apps actually work?
   **Answer Approach:** Evidence is mixed. Most lack research. Be honest about what research shows.

6. **Q:** What is the best routine app for kids with ADHD?
   **Answer Approach:** Joon, Brili, Goally. But note that routines only work when child is regulated first.

7. **Q:** Can apps replace ADHD medication?
   **Answer Approach:** No. Apps are supplementary support. Professional guidance still important.
