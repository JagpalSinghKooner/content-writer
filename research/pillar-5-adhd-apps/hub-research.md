### PAA Questions (10)

1. **Q:** Do ADHD apps really help children?
   **Answer Approach:** Honest answer: some do, for specific things. Research shows inattention improves (effect size -0.25), executive function improves more (effect size 0.71). But most apps fail after 2 weeks. Explain why and what to look for.

2. **Q:** What are the best ADHD apps for kids UK?
   **Answer Approach:** UK-specific recommendations including NHS-aligned tools. Mention QbTest for diagnosis. Categories: focus, organisation, emotional regulation, sleep, sound.

3. **Q:** Are ADHD apps safe or will they make screen time worse?
   **Answer Approach:** Address the paradox honestly. Distinguish between active screen engagement (games, scrolling) and passive sound support. Position HushAway as screen-free option.

4. **Q:** Which apps help children with ADHD focus on homework?
   **Answer Approach:** Focus apps (Forest, Pomodoro timers) work IF child can already self-regulate. If not, need to address regulation first. Introduce calm-first principle.

5. **Q:** Can apps help my ADHD child sleep?
   **Answer Approach:** Yes, research supports this. Headspace study showed reduced anxiety and sleep problems. Sound-based apps particularly effective. Position HushAway's sleep soundscapes.

6. **Q:** Are there free ADHD apps that actually work?
   **Answer Approach:** Yes - Habitica (free), Forest (free version), Choiceworks. But free often means limited. HushAway's free forever tier gives full Sound Sanctuary access.

7. **Q:** Will apps help my child with emotional regulation?
   **Answer Approach:** Yes, but mechanism matters. Mightier teaches recognition. Meditation apps build awareness. Sound apps provide immediate regulation support. Different approaches for different needs.

8. **Q:** What is the only FDA-approved ADHD app?
   **Answer Approach:** EndeavorRX - video game for attention training, ages 8-12. Prescription required, $99/month. Important to mention but also note it's active engagement, not passive support.

9. **Q:** Can meditation apps help children with ADHD?
   **Answer Approach:** Yes, but not for what parents expect. Doesn't improve hyperactivity directly but helps anxiety and sleep (Headspace study). Some children resist sitting still - sound apps are alternative.

10. **Q:** How do I know if an ADHD app is actually helping?
    **Answer Approach:** Track specific outcomes before/after. Task completion rates, morning routine time, sleep quality. App should be showing improvement within 2-4 weeks or try different approach.
