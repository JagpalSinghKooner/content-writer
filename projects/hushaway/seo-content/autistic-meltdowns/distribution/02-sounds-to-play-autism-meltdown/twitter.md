3. ASMR SOUNDS (for some children)

Gentle tapping, soft brushing, quiet rustling, slow pouring water.

### Tweet 7
HOW TO INTRODUCE SOUND:

1. Have it ready BEFORE you need it
2. Start quietly, increase gradually
3. Don't announce it (just press play)
4. Give it time (minutes, not seconds)
5. Keep it consistent (don't switch tracks)

The critical piece most parents miss:

Test sounds when your child is CALM, not during crisis.

Play them during quiet time, wind-down, before sleep.

The sounds that help when they're regulated are likely to help during overwhelm.
