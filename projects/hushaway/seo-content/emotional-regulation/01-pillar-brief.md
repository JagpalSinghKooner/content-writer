# Pillar Brief: Emotional Regulation for Children

## 1. Strategic Context

**Pillar:** Emotional regulation for children
**Priority:** Medium
**Why now:** Parents of ND children search for regulation strategies constantly, yet NO UK competitor positions sound as the PRIMARY tool. The market is saturated with breathing exercises, fidget toys, and Zones of Regulation content—but sound-based regulation is virtually absent as a systematic approach.
**Proprietary advantage:** HushAway® is the ONLY platform offering passive, zero-demand audio specifically designed for emotional regulation in neurodivergent children. Every competitor recommends tools requiring active participation (breathing, fidgets, movement). Sound requires nothing during peak dysregulation—just press play.

## 8. Key Messaging

### The Gap We Fill

**What every competitor says:**
- "Try breathing exercises"
- "Use fidget toys"
- "Create a calm corner with blankets and sensory items"
- "Practice co-regulation by staying calm yourself"

**What NO competitor says:**
- "Play THIS specific sound for THIS emotional state"
- "Sound works when other tools can't because it requires nothing"
- "Here's audio designed specifically for neurodivergent children's regulatory needs"

### Unique Mechanism

**The problem:** During peak dysregulation, children can't:
- Follow breathing instructions (requires cognition)
- Use fidgets properly (requires motor control)
- Engage with calm boxes (requires decision-making)
- Respond to parent coaching (requires receptive language)

**The solution:** Sound requires nothing. Just press play. The nervous system responds to audio input even when higher brain functions are offline. This is why passive listening is the missing tool in emotional regulation guidance.

### Research Support

While sound-based regulation is absent from parent-facing content, academic research supports it:
- Scoping review on sound interventions and stress response shows promise
- ASMR research indicates nervous system calming in sensory-sensitive individuals
- Music therapy for ND children shows measurable benefits
- Polyvagal theory explains why rhythmic, predictable sound supports regulation

HushAway® can position as the bridge between this research and practical parent guidance.
