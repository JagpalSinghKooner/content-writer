### Moshi Kids (Mind Candy Ltd.)

- **URL:** moshikids.com
- **DR Estimate:** 60-70
- **UK Position:** Dominant market leader (10M+ streams monthly, 10B+ cumulative minutes)
- **Pricing:** £39.99/year or £7.99/month (7-day free trial)
- **Angle:** Entertainment-first, passive audio consumption, "sleep in 10 minutes"
- **Strengths:**
  - UK-created (Mind Candy heritage from Moshi Monsters)
  - 400+ original audio stories, meditations, sounds, music
  - True passive listening (no interaction required)
  - Parent testimonials show ADHD/autism parents finding success
  - Celebrity narrators (Patrick Stewart, Brian Blessed, Goldie Hawn)
  - Heart-rate-synced soundtracks for sleep onset
- **Gaps:**
  - NOT designed specifically for neurodivergent children
  - No content addressing auditory sensitivities
  - Children can become dependent on app for sleep
  - Content less effective for children over age 11
  - No frequency-based sounds (solfeggio, binaural beats)
  - No ASMR content designed for ND processing

**Why HushAway wins:** Moshi is "adapted FOR" ND kids through emergent parent discovery. HushAway is "designed FOR" ND kids from the ground up. Moshi's passive listening is accidental; HushAway's is intentional and neuroscience-informed.
