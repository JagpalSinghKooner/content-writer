### Tweet 4
The problem with gradual retreat:

### Tweet 5
Here's the missing piece:

### Tweet 6
How sound addresses each fear:

**Separation:** Sound doesn't leave when you do

**Darkness:** Hearing fills the gap vision leaves

**Quiet:** External input quiets internal worries

**Control:** Predictable environment = less terror

Week 2: Sound stays as you move away
Week 3: Sound continues when you leave
Week 4: Sound becomes their safety object
