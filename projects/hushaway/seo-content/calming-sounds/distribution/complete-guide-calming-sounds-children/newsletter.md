# Newsletter Snippet: Calming Sounds for Children

## Hook

It's 9:47pm. Again. You've tried the apps, played the white noise, tested the meditation videos other parents swear by. Nothing sticks for your child.

Here's what we want you to know: it's not your child. It's not you. It's the sounds themselves.

## Body

Most calming sounds weren't designed for neurodivergent children. They were made for neurotypical brains and hoped to work for everyone. But your child's brain processes sound differently.

Research from the American Academy of Pediatrics shows that over 90% of autistic children experience sensory processing differences. Children with ADHD similarly process sensory input differently than their neurotypical peers. These differences mean generic sounds often fail.

When your child is overwhelmed, they can't follow instructions. "Close your eyes and imagine..." fails when their nervous system is in fight-or-flight. They need sounds that require nothing. Press play and done.

The difference between sounds adapted FOR neurodivergent children and sounds designed FOR them from the ground up? Parents notice it within days. Bedtime shifts from 90 minutes to 20. Meltdown recovery happens faster. Homework doesn't require three arguments.

One mum told us: "I didn't believe it would work after everything we'd tried. Within a week, bedtime was different." That's not magic. It's matching the right sounds to how your child actually processes them.
