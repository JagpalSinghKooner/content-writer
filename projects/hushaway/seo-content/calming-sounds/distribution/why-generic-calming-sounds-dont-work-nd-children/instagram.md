## Visual Notes

**Carousel Design:**
- Colours: Soft, calming palette (muted blues, gentle greens, warm neutrals)
- Fonts: Clean, sans-serif, highly readable
- Contrast: High contrast for accessibility
- Layout: Generous white space, not overwhelming
- Visual hierarchy: Bold headlines, supporting text smaller
- Avoid: Busy backgrounds, multiple fonts, sensory triggers

**Reel B-Roll Suggestions:**
- Close-up of child with headphones (covering ears vs. peaceful listening)
- Parent scrolling through dozens of apps (frustration)
- "Press play" button (simplicity)
- Child settling peacefully with sound playing
- Text overlays for three failure modes
- Calming visuals (nothing jarring or fast-moving)

**Key Visual Principle:**
The content itself should demonstrate sensory-safe design. No flashing, no overwhelming movement, no jarring transitions. Practice what we preach.
